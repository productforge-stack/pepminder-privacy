PEPMINDER PRIVACY POLICY Last Updated: January 14, 2026 1. Introduction PepMinder ("we," "our," or "the App") is a peptide tracking and reminder application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. 2. Information We Collect 2.1 Data You Provide All personal data you enter into PepMinder—including peptide information, schedules, dose logs, weight entries, and notes—is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. 2.2 Data Collected Automatically The App uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically: • Google AdMob (for free users): Collects advertising identifiers, device information, and general location (country/region) to serve relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy • Google Play Billing: When you make in-app purchases, Google processes your payment information. We do not have access to your payment details. See Google Play's Terms of Service: https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/ 2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect We do not collect: Your name, email, or contact information Health or medical records Precise location data Photos or media files Contacts or call logs Any data you enter into the App 3. How We Use Information Local App Data: Used solely to provide app functionality (tracking, reminders, calculations). Never leaves your device. Advertising Data: Used by Google AdMob to display ads to free users. Purchase Data: Used by Google Play to process premium subscriptions. 4. Data Storage and Security All peptide, schedule, and log data is stored locally on your device using encrypted database storage. We do not have access to this data. If you uninstall the App, all local data is permanently deleted unless you have created a backup. 5. Data Sharing We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except: Google AdMob: Receives advertising identifiers for ad delivery (free users only) Google Play: Processes subscription payments 6. Your Choices and Rights Opt out of personalized ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Google > Ads. Delete your data: Uninstall the App to delete all locally stored data, or use the App's settings to clear data. Backup/Export: You can export your data using the App's backup feature before deletion. Premium subscription: Upgrade to remove all advertising and associated data collection. 7. Children's Privacy PepMinder is not intended for use by children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through our App, please contact us. 8. Third-Party Services The App uses the following third-party services: Google AdMob (Advertising) Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases) Each service operates under its own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review. 9. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy within the App or on our website. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. 10. Contact Us If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: Email: support@pepminder.app 11. Disclaimer PepMinder is a tracking and reminder tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before beginning any peptide or medication regimen.
